04 · professional lens

My professional experience has moved across very different environments, but the common thread has always been people.

Door-to-door sales taught me how to speak with strangers and stay composed in uncomfortable moments. SaaS sales sharpened my ability to listen closely, follow logic, and read tone. Luxury sales taught me the importance of presentation, trust, and emotional intelligence with higher-end clients. Chief of Staff experience added structure, execution, the responsibility of keeping moving pieces aligned, and the ability to think several steps ahead while organizing around future priorities.

Across each chapter, I've learned that strong communication is rarely just about what is said. It is also timing, tone, trust, and the ability to understand what someone actually needs.
